Music

Music is something that's pretty much exclusive to the human race. It's a human activity and part of every culture in the world. After all, humans are social animals for a good reason. When people cooperate within groups, stuff gets done. Basically, when people join together and use their unique skills, talents, and knowledge for a more significant cause, much can be accomplished. For example, no single individual alone can create a rocket, fly to the moon, and then come back down to earth. It took a great many people to achieve this feat, and America realized this fact. With all that being said, it's now easier to understand how music and culture can come together. Culture is a means of ensuring stability and survival for society, and music works towards that goal by bringing people together.
